📱 How to Subscribe

Google Calendar:

  1. Open Google Calendar (web)
  2. Click + next to "Other calendars"
  3. Select "From URL"
  4. Paste the URL above
  5. Click "Add calendar"

Apple Calendar:

  1. Open Calendar app
  2. File → New Calendar Subscription
  3. Enter the URL above
  4. Click OK
